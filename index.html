What is RiskLens?

RiskLens is the economic AI layer of ZENTRA. It interprets global macro pressure, sector vulnerability, trade stress, energy shock, credit fragility and systemic disruption through a multi-lens intelligence framework.

Global Risk Monitoring Macro pressure, inflation, credit stress, energy and volatility.
Multi-Lens Analysis Macro, market, commodity, trade, sector, health, disaster and geo-economic lenses.
Decision Support Institutional interpretation, scenario reading and strategic output.
