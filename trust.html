Trust Center

ZENTRA is designed as a behavioral financial infrastructure platform. Security, compliance and responsible intelligence are core principles of the system architecture.

Security Architecture

Behavioral AML

Detection of abnormal financial flows and transaction velocity.

Fraud Signals

Merchant instability and transaction anomaly detection.

Account Misuse

Identity-behavior mismatch monitoring and detection.

KYC Intelligence

Behavior backed identity verification signals.

Compliance Principles

Explainable Intelligence

ZENTRA provides explainable signals suitable for regulated environments.

Decision Support

ZENTRA provides decision support signals, not autonomous decisions.

Institution Control

Financial institutions remain the final decision authority.

Audit Visibility

Signals can be reviewed and audited by compliance teams.

Responsible AI

ZENTRA uses AI only for signal interpretation, behavioral pattern detection and explainable insight generation. The system is designed as a decision support infrastructure rather than an automated decision maker.

Security Disclosure

If you discover a vulnerability within the ZENTRA platform, please report it responsibly.
