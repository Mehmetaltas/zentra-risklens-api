ZENTRA Platform Architecture

ZENTRA is a global economic intelligence platform that combines financial data, behavioral finance, macroeconomic signals and artificial intelligence to detect financial risk in real time.

Core System Layers

1 — Data Sources

Global financial data, macroeconomic indicators, market prices, sector signals and behavioral financial patterns.

2 — Signal Layer

Data normalization, signal extraction and economic stress indicators.

3 — Core Engines
  • Risk Engine
  • Scenario Engine
  • Sector Engine
  • Global Risk Engine
4 — Intelligence Layer

Artificial intelligence interprets economic signals and produces financial insights.

5 — Visualization Layer
  • Global Risk Map
  • Economic Radar
  • Sector Pressure
  • Risk Dashboard
6 — Application Layer
  • AI Economic Advisor
  • Investor Dashboard
  • Institutional Monitoring

Platform Modules

RiskLens Engine

Behavioral financial risk scoring engine used by financial institutions.

Credit Stress Engine

Installment and financing pressure analysis for lending systems.

Global Telescope

Macro monitoring system detecting global economic stress.

AI Economic Advisor

AI interpretation layer answering economic and financial questions.

Crisis Radar

Early detection system for economic crises and financial shocks.

Sector Intelligence

Sector pressure detection for businesses and industries.
