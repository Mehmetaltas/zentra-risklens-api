Questions Investors Will Ask

Every core layer should explain itself without requiring a presentation call.

What is ZENTRA in one sentence?

ZENTRA is a global economic intelligence operating system that observes the world economy, analyzes risk, simulates scenarios and supports financial and trade decisions.

Why are there two entry layers?

Because the public-facing AI interaction should remain separate from the protected institutional intelligence layer.

Is this only a risk dashboard?

No. ZENTRA combines risk intelligence, finance logic, simulation, trade opportunity detection and strategic observation.

Can this grow into a larger system later?

Yes. The current structure is designed to expand into cloud services, agent orchestration, role-based access and deeper AI modules.
