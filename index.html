    Security Lens — NFC / Biometric / AML Safety

    Institutions warn users not to share accounts. ZENTRA adds detection + prevention layers for illicit use, unauthorized NFC flow, and compliance visibility.

    Security

    Why it matters

    Visible

    Banks routinely warn customers: “Do not let third parties use your accounts.” Account misuse enables illegal betting, fraud, laundering, and can cause legal and financial consequences. ZENTRA’s Security Lens converts this into measurable behavioral signals and early warning flags.

    NFC + biometric confirmation concept: high-risk transactions require device-bound biometric confirmation and anomaly monitoring. This is a lens layer (not only a product feature).
