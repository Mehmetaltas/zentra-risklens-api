Architecture

Zentra is designed as an API-first modular infrastructure. Each lens can be licensed independently or bundled into enterprise deployments.

Why this matters

Most systems show dashboards. Zentra produces risk intelligence that integrates into underwriting, fraud prevention, and compliance decision flows. This creates long-term lock-in through measurable default reduction and better risk selection.

Participation (Islamic) Finance ready

Zentra can support participation finance institutions by enabling compliant risk modeling, invoice verification, and fund-based workflows without positioning Zentra as the lender. Banks/funds remain the financing party; Zentra provides decision infrastructure.
