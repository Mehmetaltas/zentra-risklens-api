Investor Questions — Answered in Context

This layer should answer the most common questions without forcing the investor to zoom, search or guess the platform logic.

What exactly is this screen?

This is the deep institutional dashboard of ZENTRA. It is the second main layer after secure entry and represents the active risk intelligence center of the platform.

How is this different from the public page?

The public page introduces the system. This deep layer shows how the system actually observes, interprets and connects economic risk with financial and trade decisions.

Does this only show data?

No. RiskLens interprets signals, identifies sector stress, connects cause-effect logic and routes the user toward finance, simulation and trade execution layers.

Why does this matter for institutions?

Institutions need more than charts. They need an operating layer that can understand pressure, compare scenarios and support controlled decision making.

ZENTRA RiskLens Dashboard is the active decision layer between raw observation and economic action. It is designed to feel materially different from the public entry and to establish institutional-grade confidence.
