Regüle Finans için Davranışsal Risk Altyapısı

ZENTRA; bankalar, katılım finans ve regülasyon yoğun fintech’ler için lisanslanabilir davranışsal risk altyapısıdır. Odak: davranışsal risk skorlama, stres modelleme ve modüler “lens” mimarisi. Global merkez hedefi: Dubai.

What it is

  • Infrastructure, not an app: API-first risk engines built for regulated finance.
  • Behavioral scoring: real-time signals (delay/position effects).
  • Stress modeling: repayment pressure and breakpoints.
  • Lens architecture: CFO/SME/Family Office/Vakıf/Macro/Cognitive.

Why it matters

  • Traditional scores are backward-looking and miss behavior under stress.
  • Regulated lenders need early warning + auditability + fast integration.
  • ZENTRA combines behavior signals with stress simulation to reduce defaults.

Primary use-cases

  • Underwriting augmentation (behavioral risk layer)
  • Early warning / portfolio monitoring
  • Installment stress modeling and affordability simulation
