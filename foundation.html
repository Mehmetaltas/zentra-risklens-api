1. Purpose

The purpose of ZENTRA is to create an enduring economic intelligence infrastructure that improves visibility across global risk, markets, trade and systemic pressure.

2. Mission

To make global economic signals, systemic risks and strategic decision inputs visible through one integrated intelligence system.

3. Long-Term Vision

ZENTRA aims to become a global economic operating layer used by institutions that require continuous visibility across finance, logistics, trade, energy and risk.

4. Principles

Data-driven
Signal-based
Risk-aware
Decision-oriented
Execution-capable

5. Governance Direction

The system should evolve with clear governance around data quality, model logic, transparency of outputs and ethical use of intelligence.

6. Structural Commitments

Build durable infrastructure, not one-off tools.
Preserve modular architecture.
Separate product layer from core intelligence layer.
Support long-term institutional use cases.
