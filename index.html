Behavioral Risk Infrastructure for Regulated Finance

ZENTRA builds licensable behavioral risk infrastructure for banks, participation finance and regulation-heavy fintechs. Focus: behavioral risk scoring, stress modeling, and modular “lens” architecture. Global hub target: Dubai.

Why this exists

  • Traditional scores are mostly backward-looking (payment history).
  • They miss behavior signals under stress and cash-flow volatility.
  • ZENTRA adds real-time behavioral risk + stress simulation, delivered as APIs.

Roadmap (Lens Architecture)

Lens modules extend the same core engine. The strategy is focus-first: perfect the scoring API, then expand.

PHASE 1 — LIVE

Behavioral Risk Scoring API

RiskLens API + Credit Stress Engine. Stabilize scoring, monitoring, and integration quality.

PHASE 2

CFO Lens + SME Lens

Cash-flow breakpoints, runway risk, FX/interest sensitivity, enterprise-grade controls.

PHASE 3

Family Office + Macro Shock Lens

Wealth erosion, concentration risk, multi-scenario macro stress and resilience modeling.

PHASE 4

Cognitive Finance Lens (Game-changer)

Behavior + psychometrics + AI co-pilot → earlier default detection than classic scores.
